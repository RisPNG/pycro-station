Pick a start date and end date, choose an output folder, and the pycro will fetch daily VCB exchange rates for the inclusive range.

Output workbook includes:
- `All Rates` flat table for every returned currency/date
- Major-currency sheets such as `USD_VND`, `EUR_VND`, `JPY_VND`, and `MYR_VND`
