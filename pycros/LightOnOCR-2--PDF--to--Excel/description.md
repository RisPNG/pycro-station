Uses LightOnOCR-2-1B, a state-of-the-art 1B-parameter vision-language model, to extract text and tables from PDF documents and convert them to Excel format. Automatically detects GPU availability and optimizes performance for discrete GPUs (CUDA), Apple Silicon (MPS), or CPU-only systems.
